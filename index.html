DJ CJ's Next Playlist

Here we have the upcoming playlist for my next gig! Audience had some reccommendations. So, like any good DJ, I obliged and curated a wonderful list of songs to keep the party jamming through the night. I was sure to give my personal opinions and ratings for all 9 on the list, enjoy!
Song and Artist Name Style of Music Date Released Country of Origin Rating and Comments
TORE UP by Don Toliver Hip-Hop/Rap June 14, 2024 USA 8/10:
A shorter song with a lot of punch. An excellent guitar performance on the under-tones with powerful percussion taking the fore-front.
Wouldn't You Like by Jorge Rivera-Herrans Pop February 13, 2024 USA 9/10:
Lots of pop and pow. A song one can easily hit the dance floor to. Beautiful piano and chorus.
Start a Riot by DUCKWRTH Rap December 14, 2018 USA 7/10:
A classic rap from Into the Spider-Verse. Start a Riot with the beat drops in this homerun beat.
Lifelight (Natsu Fuji Remix) Pop November 2, 2018 Belgium 8/10:
For the gamers out there, this remix of the Super Smash Bros. Ultimate main theme is powerful and poppy.
I'm Still Standing by Elton John Pop-Rock September 1, 2010 United Kingdom 9/10:
The discussion of what makes a pop-rock song good cannot ignore the brilliance of Elton John. Listen to one of his greatest hits of all time.
My Ordinary Life by The Living Tombstone Indie Electronic November 23, 2017 USA 8/10:
A cult classic from The Living Tombstone. Prepare to dance to some electric beats!
Let's Get It Started (Remastered) by The Black Eyed Peas Rap June 16, 2009 USA 7/10:
No DJ's playlist would be complete without The Black Eyed Peas! Remastered in the early 2000's for listeners delight.
Monkey's Paw by Cami-Cat Rock October 25, 2022 USA 9/10:
From an indie musician, Monkey's Paw is a great rock song with magnificent guitar riffs and stellar vocals.
Thunder Bringer by Jorge Rivera-Herrans Indie Rock July 4, 2024 USA 10/10:
An Absolutely amazing vocal performance by Luke Holt. The use of thunder in the song adds a level of nuance and immersion that not many songs can replicate. The brilliance of Jorge shines bright in this piece.
